Layers and @for

You can also use @for or @if to show/hide individual layers by nesting them inside the map element. In this case, you can use [leafletLayer] to bind an individual layer. 

<div leaflet style="height: 300px;" [leafletOptions]="options">
    @for(m of markers; track $index) {{ '{' }}
        <div [leafletLayer]="m"></div>
    {{ '}' }}
</div>
